Music

I started playing music at a young age. Mrs. Sneed, the music teacher at my elementary school taught me the piano from third to eigth grade. John, my grandfather, taught me the guitar when I was 12. I release music under the name Avery Island BCE. Additionally, I play bass in the bands STEEF, Action Lady, and Butte. Since moving to New Orleans in 2022, I have played in The Bloomies, Kay Weathers, Nethermead, Kelly Duplex, Eva Lovullo, Chloé Marie, Hemlock, Dusty Santamaria, and Honey the Fool.
