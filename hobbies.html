Hobbies

Hot Yoga

I practice Bikram, Vinyasa, Yin, and Restorative yoga mainly in a hot room exceeding 100°F. I would love to complete a yoga teacher training program or intensive retreat one day.

Reading

I love books about music history; anything written by George Orwell, José Saramago, Patti Smith, or Ali Smith; and surreal poetry and prose. I am currently reading "Strangers in Their Own Land" by Arlie Russell Hochschild; it aims to breach the harsh empathy wall currently dividing US politics and understand steadfast conservative opinions especially regarding big industry, pollution, and climate change.

Language Learning

One of my long-term goals is to be fluent in Spanish. I aim to focus on this romance language before exploring others since I have a tendency to mix them. I enjoy reading books like "Como Agua Para Chocolate," viewing shows such as "La Casa de Papel," taking clases at Casa de España, engaging in conversation with Spanish-speakers, and spending time on language learning apps. I also know a little French and Portuguese.

Traveling

In college, I studied abroad in Strasbourg, France, before interning at CARRASCO art gallery in Lison, Portugal, the following summer. This period of my life was the most I had ever travelled and introduced me to many new cultures and places in Europe. Last summer, I took my first US road trip West that included stops in Marfa, TX, Canyonlands in Utah, and Grand Teton in Wyoming. I now have a goal to visit all national parks. I want to visit Mexico City, Buenos Aires, or Costa Rica next.
